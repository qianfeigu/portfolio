Proposal for Posti

Service Design/ Business Design

Team project/ Jan. 2020 - Feb. 2020
The project was innitiated by the course “Design Strategy and Entrepreneurship” in Aalto. Professor Peter asked us to study different business models and strategies between startups and established companies. Our team was divided into two parts to study two companies: Posti (Finnish state-owned postal company) and Miniverso (a small local farm-based greens startup), and propose future plans for both of them considering the external environment. Here I only included Posti that I was participating.
