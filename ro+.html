Final Design

Our concept creates a hassle-free journey for Roma people. We created a platform RO+, where Roma people, healthcare providers and social service workers can access information in one place and coordinate with each other on referrals, appointments and the status of temporary ID. 

01.

Referral receiver & storage

RO+ app can extract the information from the email containing your referral and store it inside the app. You will be notified when booking appointments.

02.

Temporary ID registration & documentation

If you don't have a temperary ID, you can register one by filling in the information step-by-step. If you've already got one, you can upload yours by scanning it.

03.

Appointment & Translator booking

You can book your appointment and translator through the app, without the help from social service workers. All the suggested slots have been agreed by doctors.

04.

Verbal assistant

Each instruction can be read and explained in the language you are familiar with. So you don't have to worry about the language barrier.

Service Blueprint

Frontstage of the services

In the solution, main front stage channels are Hirundo social service workers, Global Clinic nurses and Kalasatama info desk. Coordination between these stakeholders is crucial for the system to be efficient as a whole. 

1


Roma patient choose the available time for the appointment and get a voice reminder one day before the appointment.

2


Roma patients show the referral with the QR code at Kalasatama info desk and the info desk staff will scan it. 

3


Before the due date when the presciption is no longer valid, the app sends a reminder to notify the patient.

Backstage of the services

1

Owner of the system

the City of Helsinki and Deaconess Institute collectively

2

Kalasatama IT department

responsible for generating and keeping a record of temporary ID since they are authorised to create this official proof of identity by the city. Since a temperal ID is needed for any kind of treatment, the app will ask for previous ID number during onboarding. If the patient has no temperal ID, the app would ask for related information and help generate one through IT system.

3

Kalasatama appointment system

arrange time slot suggested by the patient with doctors and translators, and send back the confirmation to patient.

4

Global Clinic

send referral to individual patients and Kalasatama hospital. When patients get the referral, they can further make appointment in the app according to the hospital included in the referral. Kalasatama will keep the referral in system to confirm the appointment.
