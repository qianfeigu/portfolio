The City of Rokast

Service Design/Game Design

Individual work / Jun.2018 - Dec.2018
“Culture shock” has been drawing people’s attention, how- ever, “reverse culture shock” is less talked about. It is a phase for those overseas education seekers who decide to come back must be going through. The outcome is a set of board game “The City of Rokast” and the matching service system provided for the users, helping them conquer the consequences brought by reverse culture shock.
