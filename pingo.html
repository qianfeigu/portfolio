Pingo Island

Product Design

Sponsored Project / Jan.2018- Mar.2018
Thanks to Cheers, an education centre for children with special needs, me and my team had this opportunity to live with autistic children. The outcome is a set of game-related product to possibly enhance their physical incoordination and their unwillingness to communicate with others. Final design was competing with other proposals for manufacturing.
