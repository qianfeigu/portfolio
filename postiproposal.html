Proposal for Posti

Service Design & Business Design

Team project/ Jan. 2020 - Feb. 2020

The project was initiated by the course “Design Strategy and Entrepreneurship” in Aalto. The aim is to study different business models and strategies between startups and established companies.
Our team studied Posti (Finnish state-owned postal company), and collaborated with another team working on Miniverso (a small local farm-based greens startup), proposed future plans for both of them considering the difference resources and scales.
