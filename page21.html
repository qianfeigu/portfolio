Service Design / UX Design

Team project/ Jan. 2020 - Ongoing
Silver Prize Winner of Service Design Challenge 2020

Our team, 5 students from Aalto University Design School participated Service Design Challenge 2020 initiated by Philips Experience Design. The goal of the project is to find ways to improve the heart health of people in Europe who are overlooked and underserved. Our project focus on “how to keep Roma population in Helsinki within the public health services”, using service design approaches leveraging minimun viable resources.

Project Overview


This is a five-month self motivated project. Our team participated the Service Design Challenge 2020 initiated by Philips Experience Design. Thanks to the help and guidance of our mentors Robin Auer, Frank Kolkman, Maytee Rodríguez from SDC, and our tutors Nuria Solsona Cabafrom Aalto Univerity.

We followed the four stages and deadlines suggested by SDC:
1. Proposal (Identify & Explore)
2. Empathise & Discover
3. Frame & Define
4. Ideate & Develop
